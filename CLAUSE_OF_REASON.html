1. BECAUSE / SINCE / AS + S + V

  • Ho Chi Minh city was flooded because it rained heavily for hours.
  • Since Dalat is famous for its cold weather, many tourists visit there every year.
  • As it was cold outside, I slept the whole yesterday.

2. BECAUSE OF/ DUE TO + Ving/Noun

  • Ho Chi Minh city was flooded because of heavy rain.
  • Many tourists visit Dalat because of its famousness.
  • The ice melts due to global warming.

CONCLUSION

  • BECAUSE / SINCE / AS + S + V: mệnh đề chỉ lý do
  • BECAUSE OF/ DUE TO + Ving/Noun: cụm từ chỉ lý do
