Loại này để diễn tả hai sự việc có ý nghĩa trái ngược. Nghĩa của nó là mặc dù.

1. ALTHOUGH / THOUGH / EVEN THOUGH + S + V

  • The plant died although I watered it everyday.
  • Although iPhone 12 is expensive, it's worth buying it.

2. IN SPITE OF / DESPITE + Ving/Noun

  • Messi joined the match despite being injured.
  • Nam got low mark on the final exam despite his hard work.
  • In spite of his tiredness, Nam cooked me a delicious meal.
  • In spite of living far from school, Hoa has never been late for school.

3. HOWEVER: tuy nhiên

  • She tried hard. However, she couldn't play the piano.
  • However hard she tried, she couldn't play the piano. (This is a special case)

4. CONCLUSION

  • "Despite" doesn't go with of --- DESPITE OF
  • Although + S + V: smệnh đề nhượng bộ
  • In spite of / Despite + Ving/Noun: cụm từ (phrases) nhượng bộ
